About Us

Avenue Auto believes in earning and keeping each customer’s trust. The word we use is fairness. Ryan, Joe and Danny genuinely care about their customers. We honestly want the best for you. Nobody likes when their vehicle to break down, this is why our service and repair guarantees four values each of our customers know and have come to expect:

✔ Correct diagnosis and repair
✔ Economical solutions for repairs
✔ Honesty
✔ Warranties on every repair

We understand our business cannot grow without your trust.

Ryan Erich | Shop Manager

Ryan has over 22 year experience in the auto business and 15 of those have been with Avenue Auto. After graduating from technical school he received specialized certification through General Motors. Upon leaving the GM dealership we are honored he has chosen to continue his career with us. Since joing the team at Avenue Auto he has continued to pursue advanced certification specializing in Diagnostics and repair. Joe has been employed at Avenue Auto for 16 years. We call Ryan and Joe the dynamic duo. Joe stared his career with Ryan being trained through General Motors.
